[MyProgress Mod Settings]

This defines how much XP is required to reach the next level. In "easy" mode, XP requirements are halved.

myprogress_difficulty (Difficulty Level) enum normal easy,normal,hard

[Progress HUD]

These settings control the appearance of the on-screen display.

The horizontal offset for the HUD from the bottom-left corner.

myprogress_hud_offset_x (HUD X Offset) int 20 0 1000

The vertical offset for the HUD from the bottom-left corner.

myprogress_hud_offset_y (HUD Y Offset) int -20 -1000 0
